Sobre mi

Come here kid, I'm going to tell you a story... -she used to say-. In this world, exist a forest, a big, deep and dark forest with a millions of roads where many souls lost themselves trying to find their way out. Once there, you can run, walk, hide, climb, swim and you can even fly if you find the right road, but exist one thing that you cannot avoid, eventually you will cross your path with someone else. Into the woods, you can find many people, some people is going to help you, others will probably want you far out of their way and others are going to use you to get something and you are going to do the same. You don't know how many people you are going to meet or what intention they have, some of those are going to be with you for a little moment, some will accompany you at the beginning but not at the eend as well as others will do it at the end but not at the beginning. You are going to trust and be disappointed and probably you are going to disappoint someone that trust in you. There are as many probabilities as people. The point is that all those souls who lose their heads go into the dark and lost themselves for only one reason: when the dark found them, they refuse to saw. Do not forget this kid: no matter where we are or how much time pass, the moment always come, we always have to go through that forest but you have something that none of them have, you can see that? -She look up at that moment and found the same star that she pointed for her that day-, no matters how deep in the woods you can go, none can take that away from you, that's me, I'm going to be with you when the dark find you, because I believe in that, I believe in you... and when you relize that you are not alone, the full sky will guide you and you are going to find your road again. -She looked at all the girls and boys around her with a single idea in her head: "We are not alone".
